 \doxysubsection*{Celeste}



 

 Celeste is a critically acclaimed 2D platformer developed by Maddy Makes Games, released in 2018. It follows Madeline, a young woman climbing the mysterious Celeste Mountain while battling inner struggles like anxiety and self-\/doubt. The game features tight platforming mechanics, challenging yet fair difficulty, and an emotional narrative. Its pixel-\/art style, atmospheric soundtrack, and accessibility options make it a standout in the genre. ~\newline
